MS Research

Quantification of Agricultural Emission: A Comprehensive Emission Inventory of Crop Residue Open Burning in Pakistan (2010-2021). This study presents an extensive emission inventory of major air pollutants resulting from crop residue burning in Pakistan over a 12-year period, addressing the critical need for accurate air quality data. Utilizing MODIS active fire data combined with district-level crop production statistics, the research achieved high-resolution spatial and temporal distribution of emissions.
